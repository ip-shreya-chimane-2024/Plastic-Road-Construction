# Plastic Road Construction Using Waste Materials

This project studies the use of waste plastic in bituminous road construction and its impact on strength, durability, and water resistance.

## Objectives
- To understand plastic road construction process
- To compare plastic roads with conventional roads
- To analyze environmental benefits

## Methodology
The process includes collection, cleaning, shredding of plastic, mixing with hot aggregates, and addition of bitumen.

## Key Observations
- Improved strength and durability
- Better water resistance
- Reduced plastic waste

## Case Study
Based on work by Rajagopalan Vasudevan and implementation in Indian cities.
